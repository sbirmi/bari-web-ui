Taboo

  1. Objective. Your team to win by scoring the highest number of points.
  2. Turn. The game is played in turns by each player in each team. Each turn is for the same duration of time; the active player should try to complete as many tasks successfully as possible.
  3. The task. The current player receives a secret word and a list of tabooed words. The player must try to get its team members to say the secret word by giving verbal clues. The catch: the player must not use any of the tabooed words!
  4. The secret word is visible to all other teams for fun and to ensure correct play.
  5. Do's and Don'ts.
    1. This game is not charades. No gestures!
    2. Use words/sentences to give hints. No sound effects. Example: mimicing engine sounds for vehicles.
    3. No spelling the word. Can't use/convey parts of the word. Example: can't use "Blank" if "Blanket" is in the secret or tabooed list.
      4. No sounds like.
    4. Can't use acronyms or expanded versions of words.
    5. Can't use immediate variants of the word such as tables, tabular, or tabled when the secreted/disallowed word is "tabled". Synonyms are okay!
    6. Stick to communicating in a language that all can understand. Duh!
    7. The player can skip the current card if desired. This awards 1 point immediately to the other team(s)
  6. Scoring. Successful/failed execution of the task is self scored by the player and on honor system. Listen to dissent expressed by other teams!
